About the SDGs

SDG 3, SDG 5, SDG 15 and SDG 16 are global goals established by the UN to promote a fairer, healthier, and more sustainable future for all. Each goal addresses social, environmental, and economic issues, encouraging actions that generate a positive impact on people's lives and the preservation of the planet.


The SDGs chosen for the Awareness Colors Project are:


  • SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-Being: ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all.
  • SDG 5 – Gender Equality: achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.
  • SDG 15 – Life on Land: protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.
  • SDG 16 – Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions: promote peaceful and inclusive societies, access to justice, and accountable institutions.

Why these SDGs

The Awareness Colors Project connects directly to these SDGs because it promotes health, prevention, and well-being (SDG 3); encourages equality and care for everyone, regardless of gender or age (SDG 5); reinforces the protection and appreciation of life and the environment, including animal care (SDG 15); and supports actions that strengthen social and community responsibility, promoting awareness and social justice (SDG 16).
